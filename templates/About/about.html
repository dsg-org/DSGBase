What is DSGBase?

DSGBase is a modern web-based system for managing personal identity data — such as full name, district, and ID number — in a clear and organized way. It offers a responsive and easy-to-use interface that works directly in the browser.

How it Works

DSGBase allows users to add, view, and organize personal information through core sections like Description, Base, and profile. Each entry includes searchable attributes, making your data easily accessible and manageable online.

Who is it for?

DSGBase is ideal for individuals or small teams looking for a simple way to organize identity-related information. It’s perfect for users who want an online database that’s lightweight, accessible, and intuitive.

Accessibility & Privacy

DSGBase runs in the browser and can store data locally or connect to a server, depending on your setup. While it's not designed for sensitive or encrypted storage, it avoids unnecessary data sharing and encourages responsible use and hosting.

Technology Stack

DSGBase is built using JavaScript, Python, and C, combining web and low-level technologies to ensure performance, flexibility, and scalability.

Created By

DSGBase was created by !!D7FEX and Gurjaka to provide a clean, online-first data management experience for anyone who values simplicity and control.
